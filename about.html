Empowering YOU

At Black Sail Editing, my mission is to empower women in national security and government roles by helping tell their stories. Whether you’re looking to refine your manuscript, need guidance through the book-writing process, or seek a trusted partner to bring your vision to life through ghostwriting, I’m here to ensure your voice is heard—strong, clear, and unforgettable. height: 40vh width: 80% horizontal-placement: center vertical-placement: center text-alignment: left scheme-name: dark-navy - id: rich-text text_html: >-

With a decade of experience in national security, I know firsthand what it takes to navigate high-stakes environments and the importance of clear, impactful communication. From cyber operations to international policy, my work has been rooted in strategy, precision, and understanding complex global issues—skills I now bring to the world of writing, editing, and coaching.

But beyond the strategy and operations, I’ve had the privilege of living abroad in South Korea and Germany, where I encountered countless women in defense, intelligence, and government sectors—each one fierce, resilient, and pioneering. It is these women’s stories that inspire me to help amplify their voices.

I also understand the importance of fantasy in transporting readers to fictional realms where they can discover new parts of themselves that they can take off the page into their every day lives.

I believe that stories have the power to not only shift narratives but to inspire future generations of women in these fields. Let’s chart a course for your story together—one that reflects your strength, your journey, and your impact on the world.
