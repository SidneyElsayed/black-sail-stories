Manuscript Evaluation

text_html: >-

Once you have a completed draft, a manuscript evaluation can provide valuable big-picture insight before diving into a full edit. I will read your manuscript and provide a detailed editorial letter outlining strengths, areas for improvement, and suggestions for revision. This feedback focuses on elements such as structure, pacing, character development, and overall clarity, giving you a clear roadmap for the next stage of your writing process.
